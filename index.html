Xiaomin Ouyang

I am a third-year PhD student at the Department of Information Engineering, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Guoliang Xing and Prof. Jianwei Huang. I also work closely with Prof. Jiayu Zhou.

My research interests include smart health, federated learning, wireless communications and networking. I obtained my bachelor’s degree (Rank 1/68) in Communication Engineering from Xiamen University in Jul. 2019.
