Xiaomin Ouyang

I am a last-year PhD student at the Department of Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Guoliang Xing and Prof. Jianwei Huang. I also work closely with Prof. Jiayu Zhou, Michigan State University.

My research interests include Artificial Intelligence for Internet of Things and Networked/Embedded Systems, with a primary focus on developing novel machine learning systems and sensing technologies for smart health applications. I am currently working on the design and deployment of a multimodal federated learning system for Alzheimer’s Disease monitoring (Project Page).

I received my B.E. degree with honor (rank 1/68) in Information and Communication Engineering, from Xiamen University, China in 2019.
